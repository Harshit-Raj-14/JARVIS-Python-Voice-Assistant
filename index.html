KNOW MORE

JARVIS (Just A Rather Very Intelligent System) is a fictional artificial intelligence assistant created by Marvel Comics and featured in the Iron Man comics and movies. JARVIS is the computer system that helps Tony Stark (Iron Man) manage his technology and equipment, from his suit to his lab. JARVIS is portrayed as an incredibly advanced and intelligent system, capable of natural language processing, learning, and problem-solving. In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, JARVIS was portrayed as a voice-activated assistant, voiced by actor Paul Bettany, before being transformed into the superhero Vision.
